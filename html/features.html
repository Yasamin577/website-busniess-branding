TrackMate Features

Expense Categorization

TrackMate simplifies expense management by allowing users to categorize their spending. With options for custom categories and manual entries, students can track every detail. Future updates will introduce automatic categorization.

Income and Expense Management

TrackMate provides a clear profit and loss summary with visual graphs and charts, along with real-time tracking of overall balance.
Users can receive notifications for significant changes and monthly reports that summarize their financial health.

Goal Setting and Tracking

Set and achieve savings goals by specifying the amount, timeframe, and purpose. TrackMate calculates required savings and tracks progress with milestone reminders, enabling students to reach their goals confidently.
